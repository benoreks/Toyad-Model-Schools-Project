Our Vision:

Our vision is to be a world class citadel of learning with a reputation for excellence and discipline in order to train-up and nurture the total child to be found worthy academically, morally, spiritually and socially.

Our Mission:

  • To be a school that offers an irresistible invitation to learning; where learning is an exciting adventure; where all learners make outstanding progress and where learning is a preparation for life.
  • To empower pupils to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will help them to participate in and contribute to the global world.
  • To provide a quality inclusive learning environment that is responsive to pupil voice.
  • To have a morally grounded, passionate and compassionate teachers.
  • To groom future leaders with great minds who would be broadened to become better citizens of the world.

Our Core Values:

  • Diligence
  • Integrity
  • Discipline
  • Respect & Self-control
